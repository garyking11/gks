I have been a recording producer, engineer, musican and studio owner. I started my first studio in 1975. "King Studios", an 8-track studio recording local bands and songwriters. After growing my studio over the years, the "House of Leisure" emerged.

HOL was mya 16/24 track analog studio located in Seattle between 1991 and 1996.  Recording mostly rock bands located. We enjoyed working with first time recording artists to world class musicians.

Acheiving some local notoriety working with artists, albums and labels such as Hater (SubPop), Hater the 2nd (Barsuk Records), Soundgarden (A&M Records), Gruntruck (Roadrunner Records), Sony Records, CZ Records, Loosegroove, Suction, Tad and many others. HOL website, listen, info, photos and more.
